**Style**: Clean educational infographic, Indian cultural aesthetic, saffron and ivory color palette, white background.

**Content**: A vertical bar chart or staircase diagram showing the four states of consciousness as ascending levels of inner depth. From left to right:
1. "जागृती (Wakefulness)" — shortest bar, label "बाह्य संपर्क पूर्ण / Full sensory contact", color: teal/green.
2. "स्वप्न (Dream)" — medium bar, label "अंशतः आंतरिक / Partially internal", color: gold.
3. "सुषुप्ती (Deep Sleep)" — taller bar, label "विश्रांती / Rest", color: purple.
4. "तुरीय / समाधी (Turiya)" — tallest bar with an infinity symbol at top, highlighted in saffron, label "परम आनंद / Supreme Bliss".
