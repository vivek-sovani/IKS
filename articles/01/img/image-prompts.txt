# Image / Infographic Prompts for Article 01 — आनंद / Ananda

## Prompt 1: Mechanism Diagram (for NotebookLM or AI image tools)

**Style**: Clean educational infographic, Indian cultural aesthetic, saffron and gold color palette, white background.

**Content**: A circular diagram with "आनंद (Ananda)" at the center in a glowing golden circle. Four pathways pointing inward toward the center:
1. LEFT-TOP: "ज्ञानेंद्रिये (Jnanendriya)" — five sense organs represented by icons: eye, ear, nose, tongue, hand. Color: teal/green.
2. LEFT-BOTTOM: "कर्मेंद्रिये (Karmendriya)" — five organs of action represented by hand, foot, mouth, etc. Color: purple.
3. RIGHT-TOP: "स्मृती (Smriti)" — a swirling memory symbol. Color: gold.
4. RIGHT-BOTTOM: "निद्रा (Nidra / Sleep)" — crescent moon. Color: gray-blue.

Each pathway has a curved arrow flowing toward the central "Ananda" circle. The design conveys that all four pathways lead to inner joy. Devanagari script labels on each node. Subtitle below: "आनंदाचा मूळ स्रोत आपल्यात आहे."
